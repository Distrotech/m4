There are two mailing lists for GNU m4

m4-forum@seindal.dk

This list is intended for discussions between people interested and/or participating in the further development of m4.

m4-announce@seindal.dk

Announcements regarding GNU m4 will posted here.

The volume will certainly be very low.

Currently these lists are maintained manually. Send a message to rene@seindal.dk saying whether you want to subscribe or unsubscribe to any of these lists.
